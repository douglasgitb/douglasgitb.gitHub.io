the right yoke at the wrong price

 

Next up was to find the correct yoke to convert the raster TV to a vector monitor. Fortunately I have a Star Wars cockpit with a factory 25” Amplifone I could check. The internet, VectorList, and my garage all agreed that the yoke used on the 25” Amplifone was the same as those used on the 19” Wells-Gardner 19K6100. They are marked TRIAD-UTRAD with part number 9A2806-001D on the cockpit, and 9A2806-001A for the Wells, along with what I think is a date code (17-8234 and 17-8135) and MADE IN THE U.S.A. I think the letter suffix on the part number determines the length of the yoke wires, as the cockpit has nice long ones and the WG are shorter.

Great. Where to get one? I bought one via eBay but it turned out to be for a G08. I was not about to remove one from my Tempest or Space Duel or spare 6100.

Until the day when the best of the three (least screen burn) was out of its cab for troubleshooting and had a mishap. Crack. Hiss. Donor.
