the future

 

This has been a very exciting and gratifying experiment; I just wish the 6100 tube that gave its life was the one with the Tempest burn.

In fact, I may continue the experiment by removing the 6100 yoke and rings and test fitting that extra yoke I have for a G08. The reason? To try to upgrade my Star Trek Captain’s Chair to a 25” monitor! The first question to be answered is if the tube would fit into the cabinet. After that, It should be a very similar process to the one shown in these pages. Of course there are no 25” G08 monitors to use for reference, but the 19K6100 and the G08 are known to use the same model picture tube (100 degree 19VLUP22). Since the yoke from a G08 fits on a 6100, and the 6100 yoke fits this tube, then the G08 should also fit this tube.

I’m not in a hurry to try this for several reasons. It may not fit. The tube in my Star Trek is in very good shape. Also, the convergence is dialed in pretty well and the tube in my Star Wars has a small spot burnt in the phosphor, so maybe that’s where it will go. I know it won’t fit in an upright cabinet (I was spoiled by playing a few games of Tempest and Gravitar on a 25” Amplifone screen). Would be nice to see Major Havoc. We’ll see - but it may take another few years.
