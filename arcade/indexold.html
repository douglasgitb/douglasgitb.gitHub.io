Information and news about the Gauck boys' arcade and www.gauck.com arcade projects

Current status (Dec, 10, 2012):

Some games have been moved into the basement. The walls were insulated with spray foam and it's nice and dry and warm down there. Still need to run permanent electric but may skip the drywall for now as the foam gives it a lava cave-like appearance (albiet green). Plans are for some kind of carpet, controllable lighting levels, blacklight features (wall flecks and/or carpet) and a small disco ball. The layout plan is still the same. Have the vector row in place as well as Championship Sprint and the Star Trek cockpit. Star Wars and Omega Race may not fit down the outer stairs and I haven't moved the pinball yet as I need more muscle.

The Joust cabinet will be a Multi-Williams (converted back from Hydra) and the Galaxian will be a vertical multi (using the ArcadeSD board). Anyone have a SketchUp model for Omega Race or Star Trek cockpit?
